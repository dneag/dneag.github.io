I've been chipping away at this for years. It's a program for generating tree meshes, currently in the form of a command plugin for Autodesk Maya. It attempts to simulate trees' natural growth processes from start to finish. To the right is a cook pine tree. All mesh components, including uv's, were generated by the command.
